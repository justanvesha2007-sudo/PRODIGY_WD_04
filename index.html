About Me

Hi! I'm Anvesha Singh, a second year Computer Science student who believes every great idea starts with curiosity. Whether I'm building interactive web applications, exploring new technologies or collaborating with people from different cultures, I'm always looking for opportunities to learn and create.

My journey goes beyond coding. From representing my school in state level basketball to representing India at an international winter school on nuclear science in Russia, I've learned that growth happens when you step outside your comfort zone. Those experiences have shaped how I approach challenges—with discipline, adaptability and a willingness to keep learning.

As a frontend developer, I enjoy turning ideas into responsive, user-friendly experiences that are both functional and visually engaging. Every project I build is another step in my mission to grow as a developer, solve meaningful problems and create technology that people enjoy using.

When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me learning something new, exploring emerging technologies or planning my next big challenge.
